Hydrapeeling/Hydrafacial




Hydrapeel technologies were first developed in Israel. This combines the benefits of oxygen and water at supersonic speed.
Hydra peeling is a new treatment for skin rejuvenation.
It uses saline or water at the speed of around 200 km per hour to remove the top layers of the skin and produce collagen remodelling.
Hydrapeel machines are also used to remove superficial lines and wrinkles.The saline water can be mixed with peel solutions.
Hydrapeel techniques were developed recently and it can also be combined with chemical peels and dermarollers for added benefits.
Hydrafacials are very safe with almost no side effects. Dr Sachith was one of the first dermatologists to adopt this new technology.
Jet-peel can be combined with other facial rejuvenation techniques like derma roller, peels, microdermabrasion and facelift procedures.

The conditions it treats are:

  • Acne pigmentation
  • Wrinkles and fine lines
  • Superficial lines
  • Pigmentation
  • Aging skin.
